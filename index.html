How to use this app
When you first load or reload the page, you'll notice that the 'Enter Next Y Coordinate' field is pre-filled with the value 4.

Simply click the green 'Add' button to append this value to the graph below. The RNN will then predict the rest of the graph, shown in blue.

To reset the graph, click the red 'Reset' button. Keep in mind that you'll need to add 10 new values to the graph before the RNN AI can make accurate predictions again.

What is a RNN?
RNN, short for Recurrent Neural Network, is a predictive machine learning model known for its ability to leverage past predictions for enhanced accuracy.

Like Deep Neural Networks (DNNs), RNNs are composed of nodes interconnected with weights and biases.

They find applications in a wide range of domains including weather forecasting, stock market analysis, and any scenario where trend prediction is crucial.
